Welcome on Squid game marbles !

Click on the « start » button to start.

Then choose the number of balls you want to bet by clicking on a ball from the left.

Then choice between "oven" or "odd".

Wait...

Then keep your opponent choose his own marbles et bet if you've a number of marbles even or odd in your hand.

If you choose the "pair" button and the opponent has an odd number of balls in his hand you lose the turn: in this case the opponent takes the number of balls he has bet from you. And vice versa.

The first one who has no more marbles loses.
